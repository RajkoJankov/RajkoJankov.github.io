Assassin's Creed Odyssey

Short info

Developed by Ubisoft Quebec and released by Ubisoft , the game is 11th title in AC main series. Gameplay in this title is more like a rpg game than that of an action game used for most AC titles.Anyhow , this one takes you in ancient Greece, and being an outcast descendant from Spartan legend Leonidas you make a decision who lives and who dies.
