Fallout 76

Short info

From game series that was previously being developed by Black Isle and now by Bethesda, there comes a title called Fallout 76. It marks 5th title in main series and as far as we have been shown it is a prequel to first title and is being made as mostly online-multiplayer game. Grouping up with others to make your own setlement and explore wastelands and occupy or destroy other towns until someone destroy yours... it is an expirience to behold.
