Death Stranding

Short info

Developed by Kojima Studios and released by Sony Interactive Entertainment this game is is a first title that famous game maker Hideo Kojima is making since he left Konami. Many people compare this upcoming title with Metal Gear Solid franchise but will this game be a copy of a famous game or will it be something new to look upon , only time will tell. What can i say is that so far the game looks good but will it be a hit with real actors appearing as characters or will it fail like so many games that tried same thing before ???
